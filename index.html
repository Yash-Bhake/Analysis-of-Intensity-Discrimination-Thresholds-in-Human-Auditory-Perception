Intensity Discrimination

Psychoacoustic Threshold Assessment

Research Ethics & Consent

Study Information

You are invited to participate in a psychoacoustic research study investigating intensity discrimination thresholds using a 2-Alternative Forced-Choice (2AFC) paradigm.

What You Will Do

You will listen to pairs of tones and identify which one sounds louder. The experiment will take approximately 10-15 minutes to complete.

Requirements

• You must use headphones or earphones

• You should be in a quiet environment

• You must be 18 years or older

• You should have normal or corrected-to-normal hearing

Risks & Benefits

This study involves listening to tones at comfortable volume levels. There are no known risks beyond those encountered in everyday life. Your participation will contribute to our understanding of auditory perception.

Data & Privacy

Your responses will be recorded anonymously. No personally identifiable information will be collected. Data will be used for research purposes only.

Voluntary Participation

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty.

Participant Information

Important: Please ensure you are using headphones or earphones in a quiet environment before proceeding.
