Intensity Discrimination Study

Psychoacoustic Research - Stage 2

Research Participation Consent

Study Purpose

You are invited to participate in a psychoacoustic research study investigating intensity discrimination thresholds across different frequencies and tone types.

What You Will Do

You will complete 8 blocks of listening trials (approximately 60-90 minutes total). In each trial, you will hear two tones and identify which one sounds louder.

Session Structure

• 8 blocks of approximately 25-40 trials each

• Mandatory 2-minute rest breaks between blocks

• Please complete all 8 blocks in one session

Requirements

• You must use headphones or earphones

• You should be in a quiet environment

• You must be 18 years or older

Voluntary Participation

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time.

Participant Information

We would like to record your name with your data. This is optional - if you prefer to remain anonymous, leave the field blank and we will assign you a random ID.

Volume Calibration

Click the button below to play a calibration tone. Adjust your system volume to a level that is loud but comfortable. Once set, do not adjust your volume during the experiment.

Experiment Instructions

Your Task

  • In each trial, you will hear two tones separated by a brief silence
  • Click the "Play Tones" button when you are ready to listen
  • You can replay the tones if needed
  • Identify which tone was louder: Sound 1 or Sound 2
  • Once you select an answer, the next trial will begin

Important Guidelines

  • Focus your full attention when playing the tones
  • Take your time - there is no rush
  • If unsure, make your best guess
  • You will complete 8 blocks with mandatory 2-minute breaks between them
  • Please complete all 8 blocks in one session (approximately 60-90 minutes)
